Signs You Need a Roof Replacement

  • Leaks: Water stains on your ceilings or walls are a clear sign your roof needs attention. Don't wait — leaks get worse and more expensive fast.
  • Missing Shingles: Bare spots on your roof expose the underlayment to weather damage. Address them as soon as possible.
  • Sagging: If your roof begins to sag, the support structure is weakening. This is a serious problem that needs to be addressed immediately.
  • Age: Most asphalt shingle roofs last 20-30 years. If yours is approaching that range, a replacement is more cost-effective than repeated repairs.
  • Storm or Hail Damage: NWA weather is tough on roofs. Significant storm or hail damage often warrants a full replacement — and we work with your insurance company.
